About Dr. Rachida Parks

Dr. Rachida Parks is an Award-Winning Scholar, thought leader, and expert in digital technologies and operationalization of AI in healthcare. A tenured Associate Professor of Business Analytics and Information Systems at Quinnipiac University with a joint appointment at the Frank H. Netter School of Medicine. her expertise lies at the intersection of digital technologies (digital transformation, analytics, and AI) and healthcare (clinical, operational and financial).

Her work is published in numerous premier scholarly journals such as the European Journal of Information Systems, International Journal of Medical Informatics, Computers & Education, Decision Sciences Journal of Innovative Education; as well as in leading conferences such the Americas Conference on Information Systems, the International Conference on Information Systems, and the Decision Science Institute. Her research has been nominated and awarded internationally-competitive research awards. She recently received a US Fulbright research award to investigate operationalization of AI in healthcare in Morocco.

Dr. Parks delivers expert insights and delivers keynote addresses on a national and global scale, specializing in the domains of digitalization, digital transformation, and AI. As a leading expert, Dr. Parks offers valuable insights and strategic guidance on how these technologies can be harnessed to drive positive outcomes in the healthcare sector. Dr. Parks speaks five languages (English, French, Arabic, Spanish and Italian)
